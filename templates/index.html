About CS 1.6 MDL Format

  • Maximum 2048 vertices per model
  • Maximum 4080 triangles
  • Maximum 128 bones
  • 8-bit indexed BMP textures only
  • Hard vertex weighting (no soft skinning)

Important Notes

  • Complex materials will be converted to basic ones
  • Animations may be optimized or truncated
  • Large models will be automatically decimated
  • Textures will be resized to 512x512 maximum
